Appendix

  • a - Amino acid 3D models and AI peptide generation
  • b - Meta's Evolutionary Scale Model for quick folding and PDB generation
  • c - AlphaFold 3 Server
  • d - XML generator for 3D protein viewer
  • e - CastP: find functional pockets in proteins by looking at surface topography
  • f - DeepFRI: identify functions of proteins using a neural network
  • g - RosettaFold Diffusion to design peptides that will bind to proteins
  • h - GRAMM docking: predict how your designed peptides bind to your protein target
  • i - GRAMM output modifier to work with binder activity
  • j - Alphafold 2: Easy to use protein structure and complex prediction
  • k - Generate Chroma: experimental protein folding using diffusion models (Image from Chroma)
  • l - ESM3 Motif: Choose a functional section of a protein and build a protein with just that function
  • m - ESM3 function: Puzzle an incomplete protein sequence back to a full protein - masked input sequence and a function desciption as clues
  • n - ProteinMPNN: Solve a protein structure's backbone coordinates back to amino acid sequence
